jump_azazel

T4 Demo | T3 Soldier

This was my entry to Jump Jam 2022. I got sick with covid about halfway into the allotted time (I think it was like 10-12 days to make a map) so I was definitely low effort mapping, but I still managed to produce something pretty good. I went with generic recolours of happygonk textures and a nice skybox to substitute lack of actual detailing. It's 18 jumps long and fairly straightforward pogo with tele connectors. It ended up winning the demo category for the Jam.

Azazel has 1bonus, which features slant walls with playerclip over it at a steeper angle. This has the effect of increasing playerclip depth depending on how high up on the slant you are. Sadly this turned into a bounce airpogo wr.
