jump_achlys

T2 Demo | T1 Soldier

Achlys is my submission for Jump Jam 2023 and its definitely my best map so far. I wanted to make more easy content considering most of my maps are T5-6, and I also wanted to make more speedrun oriented content. Achlys is once again an 8 jump map, and is my first map with actual connectors. The entire map was made in 10 days and it ended up winning Jump Jam 2023. It's also got 3 secrets hidden around the map, where if you hit the all the hidden crystals a door to the bonuses open up.

Achlys has 2 bonuses. B1 is a long T4 pogo jump styled after jump_armature I did for a collab in 2021 that never ended up happening. B2 is a T1 notele bonus that's a winding displacement tunnel with really gross material proxy dev texture that moves as you move. There was going to be a third bonus that was a wallbug strafe maze type bonus but it was deemed too rat to zone.
